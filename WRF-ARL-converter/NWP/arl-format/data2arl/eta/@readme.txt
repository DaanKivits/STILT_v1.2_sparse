./data2arl/eta  			Last Revised: 03 Sep 2002 
----------------------------------------------------------------------
This directory contains a grib decoder for NOAA's ETA model grib files
with data on pressure surfaces on various grids as indicated below:

	eta04arl - eta decoder on 04 km grid
	eta12arl - eta decoder on 12 km grid
	eta40arl - eta decoder on 40 km awips grid #212
